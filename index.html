What Angel does

Angel watches for patterns and understand screen activity over time.

All parental control tools are built on screen-time limits and limited content filters. But serious risks (grooming, bullying, depression, etc.) develop over time through pressure, secrecy, manipulation, exclusion, or harmful escalation. Angel is built to notice those dynamics over time, and detect worrying changes in patterns and behavior. We understand what happens on screen.

Understands context

Angel is designed to understand what is visible on screen, who is involved, and how an interaction is changing. You can limit how much time your child is spending on Youtube, but do you know what he or she is watching?

Remembers patterns

People, topics, apps, and repeated signals can matter more than any single message or page. We can detect patterns of desocialization from the evolution of conversations over whatsapp, emails and other channels, for instance. We see changes in behaviors, relationships, content consumption.

Alerts with care

Parents should not see everything. Angel is not a spytool. It's a companion your child should trust, and an insurance for parents. We will escalate only real risks to parents, accompanied with a suggested course of action. A

How it works

A calm safety layer for the screen-native generations.

01

See the screen context

Angel brings all the features traditional monitoring apps offer, but adds a fundamental layer: we understsand all visible screen activity: videos, messages, emails, text, sound.

02

Look for concerning dynamics

Our AI has memory and builds a dynamic understanding of the galaxy of relationships around your child. True digital risk is not one time exposure, it builds up over time. Our product focuses on behavioral signals: pressure, secrecy, coordinated exclusion, boundary pushing, growing obsession, or harmful escalation. We detect risk before it's too late.

03

Escalate only when it matters

Angel's parent experience is designed around high-threshold, explainable alerts rather than constant surveillance. We treat data protection and confidentiality with the utmost importance. We pledge to protect children's intimacy, and to help parents and educators get the information they need to act, only when needed.

The parent experience

We help you get more piece of mind.

Trust principles

Protection, not surveillance.

Not a surveillance feed

Angel is not designed to show parents every message, click, or moment, or help you spy on your children.

Not a diagnostic tool

Angel surfaces safety signals for human follow-up. It does not label children or make medical conclusions. It identifies patterns so you can do your job as a parent.

Built around community

We want to build our ethical framework together with our community. As parents, we have authority, but children's private life should be respected. We'll start calibrating our approach with you during the Friends&Family Beta.
